Welcome to SpeakEasy!

SpeakEasy is a classroom-based app designed to help YOU get better at public speaking! Here's some tips on how to use SpeakEasy so that you can start speaking easy.

You will be matched with another student based on your strengths and comfort level with speaking. You MUST practice once with your first partner. Your other matches will be unlocked after this achievement!

The messages tab will bring you to your ongoing chats with other students and your teacher.

The settings tab will allow you to change your profile picture and enroll in additional classes.

You can review feedback given by your partners in Previous Partners.

If you need a reminder of any of this information, just click the information tab to the right of the Home header.
